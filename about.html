Mahsa Azimi, visual artist,

is the founder of Paint Paradise Art Studio. Her dream is for Paint Paradise to be a creative community space that inspires a love for creating art while bringing out each personâ€™s inner artist. She had a vision to create a paint party business where it was not only hosted within her studio but also mobile and could travel to any venue. Mahsa has over a decade of experience in creating contemporary and abstract art. She is passionate about painting on canvas and her media of choice is acrylic. Colours, geometric shapes, and abstract designs inspire her style. Her paintings mainly demonstrate vibrant colours, movement, and dancing. She has been a child and youth program facilitator by profession where art is always used as a method of expression and therapy.
